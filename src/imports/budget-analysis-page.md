## 🎨 Figma Make Prompt — Budget Analysis Page

Design a **Budget Analysis page** for a fintech SaaS platform called **Startawy**, a platform that helps startup founders analyze their finances and receive business insights.

### Design Style

Use a **modern fintech dashboard design** with a **teal color palette and its shades** as the primary color.

Color palette:

* Primary: Teal
* Secondary: Light teal
* Accent: Dark teal
* Background: White and soft gray

Use **clean layouts, rounded cards, soft shadows, and modern typography** similar to financial SaaS dashboards.

---

# Page Layout

Create a **dashboard-style page with a left sidebar navigation and a main content area**.

### Sidebar Navigation

Include the following menu items:

* Dashboard
* Budget Analysis
* AI Chatbot
* Market Reports Library
* Book Consultant Session
* My Sessions
* Financial Performance
* Upload Financial Data
* Feedback
* Profile
* Logout

---

# Budget Analysis Form

Create a **multi-step financial form** where startup founders enter their business financial data.

### Section 1 — Business Information

Inputs:

* Business Name
* Industry / Sector
* Business Stage (dropdown: Idea, Startup, Growing)
* Business Location

---

### Section 2 — Revenue Information

Inputs:

* Product / Service Price
* Estimated Monthly Sales
* Expected Monthly Revenue

---

### Section 3 — Fixed Costs

Inputs:

* Rent
* Salaries
* Software Subscriptions
* Utilities
* Other Fixed Costs

---

### Section 4 — Variable Costs

Inputs:

* Cost per Product
* Raw Materials
* Shipping Costs
* Packaging Costs

---

### Section 5 — Marketing Budget

Inputs:

* Monthly Marketing Budget
* Advertising Spending

---

### Section 6 — Investment Information

Inputs:

* Initial Investment
* Available Monthly Budget
* Loans (optional)

Include a **Generate Budget Analysis button**.

---

# Budget Analysis Results Section

After submitting the form, display results in **financial cards and charts**.

### Financial Summary Cards

* Total Revenue
* Total Expenses
* Net Profit / Loss
* Profit Margin

---

### Charts

Include placeholders for:

* Revenue vs Expenses chart
* Cost breakdown pie chart
* Profit trend chart

---

### AI Recommendations Section

Display automated insights such as:

* Reduce unnecessary fixed costs
* Adjust product pricing
* Increase marketing allocation

Show these recommendations in **highlighted insight cards**.

---

# Additional UI Elements

Include:

* Progress bar for the multi-step form
* Financial data cards
* Charts placeholders
* Download report button
* Save analysis option

Ensure the design looks like a **professional fintech financial analysis dashboard for startup founders**.
