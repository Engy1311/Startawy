## **Startawy Platform – System Scenario**

The Startawy platform is designed to connect startup founders with financial consultants to support business development and funding opportunities. The system includes three main types of users: **Startup Founder, Financial Consultant, and Admin**. Each user interacts with the platform through different pages and functionalities depending on their role.

### **User Registration and Login**

When a new user visits the Startawy platform, they first access the **Home Page**, where they can learn about the platform and its services. From there, the user can navigate to the **Sign Up page** to create a new account.

During registration, the user enters their personal information such as **full name, email address, password, and phone number**, and selects their role in the system (Startup Founder or Financial Consultant). After successful registration, the user can access the **Login page** to enter the platform using their email and password.

Once logged in, the user is redirected to their **Dashboard**, where they can access the features available for their role.

---

## **Startup Founder Scenario**

When a user registers as a **Startup Founder**, they gain access to features that help them manage their startup and seek financial guidance.

From the **Founder Dashboard**, the founder can navigate to the **Create Startup Profile page**. In this page, they enter startup details such as the **startup name, industry sector, business stage, founding date, country, and a description of the business**.

After creating the startup profile, the founder can view and manage their startup through the **Startup Profile page**, where they can edit startup information if needed.

If the founder is looking for financial support, they can go to the **Funding Request page** and submit a new funding request. In this page, they provide information such as the **requested funding amount, purpose of funding, and additional notes**.

Founders can also access the **Consultants page**, where they can browse available financial consultants on the platform. From there, they can view consultant profiles in the **Consultant Profile page**, which includes the consultant’s expertise, experience, and background.

The founder may also use the **Messaging or Communication page** to interact with consultants, ask questions, or discuss financial strategies.

Additionally, founders can manage their personal account through the **Profile Settings page**, where they can update their personal information or change their password.

---

## **Financial Consultant Scenario**

Users who register as **Financial Consultants** use the platform to offer financial guidance to startups.

After logging in, consultants access their **Consultant Dashboard**, where they can view startups that are seeking guidance or funding.

Consultants can visit the **Startup Listings page**, where all startups registered on the platform are displayed. From there, they can open a specific startup through the **Startup Details page** to review its information, industry, and business stage.

Consultants can also access the **Funding Requests page**, where they can review requests submitted by startup founders. This helps them understand the financial needs of different startups.

When consultants want to provide guidance, they can send **feedback, recommendations, or advice** to founders through the **Communication or Messaging page**.

Consultants also have a **Consultant Profile page**, where they can manage their professional information such as **area of expertise, experience, and availability**.

They can update their information at any time through the **Edit Profile page**.

---

## **Admin Scenario**

The **Admin** is responsible for managing and monitoring the entire platform.

After logging in through the **Admin Login page**, the admin accesses the **Admin Dashboard**, where they can view overall platform activity.

From the dashboard, the admin can navigate to the **User Management page**, where they can view all registered users, including founders and consultants. The admin can also update or remove user accounts if necessary.

The admin can also access the **Startup Management page**, where all startup profiles are displayed. This allows the admin to monitor startups registered on the platform.

Additionally, the admin can review platform activity through the **Funding Requests Management page**, where all submitted funding requests are listed.

Through these pages, the admin ensures that the platform operates correctly and that all users follow the system rules.

---

## **System Interaction Summary**

Through these different roles and pages, the Startawy platform creates an environment where startup founders can present their businesses and request financial guidance, while financial consultants can review startups and provide professional advice. The admin ensures the platform runs smoothly by managing users and monitoring system activity.
